All challenges done for this summo ball game. Use WASD to move, and push out the enemy balls. There are 5 enemies and 3 bosses. Enemies spawn randomly and increase by 1 on each enemy wave. Bosses spawn randomly per every 3 enemy waves you clear. there are 3 powerups you can get, push power, knockback aoe jump when pressing space, multi fire projectiles against all enemies on powerup pickup. As for what the bosses do. One is simply fast. One pulls you towards itself every couple of seconds, the boss color changes to gold during the pull effect). One is heavy and very slow but spawns normal enemies randomly, and for every 3 enemies spawned it also spawns a random powerup.
